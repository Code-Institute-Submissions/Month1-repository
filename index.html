Barber Black Sheep is the Ultimate in Limerick Barbering Skills and Techniques. We constantly keep up to date with trends and fashions to keep you looking your freshest and sharpest.The ultimate luxury for both scoundrels and gentlemen alike, is just waiting for you.

Barber Black Sheep prides itself on impeccable service and extensive knowledge in the craft of men's haircutting and bespoke shaving.



The Job Duties of Barbers



Today’s barbers are experts at what they do, and the clients they serve expect it. The work not only involves executing a precise haircut, but also performing any number of techniques to the face, scalp, beard, and mustache.

Barbers trim, cut, shape, and taper hair using scissors, razors, clippers, and combs using years of skill and training and a keen eye that only comes with experience. They can also often provide facial hair maintenance, custom shaves, and a number of grooming services.

About us

Theres No School like the Old school. With over 20 years experience we offer you a blend of old and new styles to create a unique and bespoke cut especially for you. At Barber BlackSheep our vision is to provide a personal and tailored service with a view to building a lifelong relationship with our clientele and community.

At Barber BlackSheep our focus is on the client and his specific needs. At Barber BlackSheep we aim to become a trusted and respected part of a regenerated community, known for value and quality service for many years to come.Our ethos is to be a step up from your regular barber shop: we like to do consultations with our customers, take our time with the cut and get it right.

We actually encourage our customers to bring photos of cuts they like and be as fussy as they want to make sure they leave happy in the end.

We also cater for the all important occassions from the First Hair Cut for your little man (with a Certificate for your memory box), to looking after The Groom and his Groomsmen for Wedding Parties (because its not just the Ladies that have to look good on that special day)
